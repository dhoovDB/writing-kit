# Example Input: Raw bullet points

**Task given to Claude:** "Write the introduction paragraph for a PRD about
a new credit limit decisioning system. Here are my notes:"

---

**Product name:** Meridian

**What it is:** The internal platform that handles real-time credit limit
decisions for our prepaid and credit card customers. When a customer tries
to use their card, Meridian decides whether the transaction is within their
limit and whether to approve or decline.

**Why it matters:**
- Processes about $250B in annual transaction volume
- Bad decisions cost us money two ways: false declines lose revenue and
  damage customer trust; over-approvals increase credit loss exposure
- Customers interact with their credit limit constantly without realizing it —
  it's invisible when it works, catastrophic when it doesn't
- Current system was built in 2017 and makes decisions using static rules;
  we want to move to dynamic, ML-driven decisioning

**Audience:** Mixed — engineering leads, risk team, product leadership
